Interior

Primitive hut is simple structure oh architecture and it is the basic for today’s architecture technology.
Also, the interior is as important as structure.
We can’t stay relaxedly without calm and good atmosphere room.
From the primitive style point of view, natural,minimal and valuable is the key word for making primitive style interior.

D . I . Y

DIY plays an important role in daily interior trending and knowing interior history .

Do It Yourself

The word “D.I.Y” coming from “Do It Yourself” was born in the middle of 20s century England.
After the World War II, military soldiers who hoped the reconstruction of hometown stood up to start working.
It is said to be the original of this word and their works were spreading to America.
The passion for renovating house and the mind to keep old but valuable things came down to people today.
It is connected to minimal and antique/vintage ideas.

minimalist

In Primitive interior , the idea “less is more” is important. Also, DIY will let you create simple,useful and efficient interior that you imagined.

Wabi-Sabi

Wabi-Sabi interior is minimalist interior related to Japanese Culture such as Rikyu’s green-tea room.
To live in comfortably, it is important to cut off unnecessary things and keep it simple just to put tables chairs.
One natural wall color will let your room refined.

antique vs vintage

In primitive-style, it is important to save and keep to use old things and respect them.

Antique-style

When you hear the word “antique”,what will you imagine?
Antique is said to be the collection over 100 years past but there’s no certain meaning for that.
In primitive style, antique is not valuable or expensive or classical art but things which is inherited from ancestor.
There’s important meaning to come down over long years.
The interior of primitive antique collections is antique style.

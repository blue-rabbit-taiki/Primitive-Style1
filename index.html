What is Primitive style ?

Hearing the words "Primitive Style",what will you imagine?
The difinition of primitive is "relating to, denoting, or preserving the character of an early stage in the evolutionary or historical development of something" and the difinition of style is a "distinctive appearance, typically determined by the principles according to which something is designed".
To put it simply,Primitive Style means Original of Design.In the architecture field,I defined the word "Primitive style" as a new word.
I hope Primitive Style is going to spread all over the place...

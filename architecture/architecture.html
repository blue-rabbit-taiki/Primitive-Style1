Architecture

As you might know ,there are several types of architecture all over the world.
They are very unique depending on the region.From South to North,the climates are different and people have been thinking of the proper style for living.
We can see the interesting details for every primitive architectures.

history

From thousands of years ago, the culture and technology of architecture have been developed until today…

Origin of the Primitive Hut

It is said that the primitive-hut is the origin of architecture and its practice.Marc-Antoine Laugier developed the theory.
In the theory, he argued for the simplicity of architecture, that architecture must return to its origins, the simple rustic hut.
He thought they should be minimal and we should respect nature.

Primitive Hut

Log Primitive Hut

The log house is one of the primitive hut made of logs.
To pile them up and consist the form.From floor to roof, all the materials are wood so it’s warm and we can stay in the house comfortably.There are several sizes of log house from small to large.

    • Earth Bag Primitive Hut

    Earth bag house is new structure of architecture in recent years.
    It is made of a lot of soil and sand bags.
    Like log house, earth bag house is build to pile up sand bags.
    After pilling them up, it’s important to apply mortar on outside of the wall.
    We can see beautiful paintings both outside and inside of the house.

    • Stone Primitive Hut

    In the hard climate region like in Arizona where the temperature is high and there is boundless desert, we can see a lot of these types of house.
    The stone house prevent people who lives there from strong solar radiation in summer and cold wind in winter.
    The ancestor of this region developed the technology how to build this stone house.
